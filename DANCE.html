DANCE

CLASSES

At LaurenHill Academy, students can take Dance as a course from Secondary 1 to Secondary 5.

Each student has a chance to try different styles, eventually choosing one to perform in the class.

TROUPE

Students may choose to join an Award-winning Dance Troupe.

They will have the opportunity to compete with other schools from across Quebec and play in dance performances.

TEACHERS
Mrs C. Ranger Teaches solely at Senior Campuses
Mrs B. Lloyd-Hughes Teaches solely at Junior Campus

STREAMS
Intro To Dance Dance: Advanced
If your child has some experience in dance (e.g. contemporary, ballet, tango) this is the one you should pick If your child has a lot of experience in dance or is in Secondary 2+ and has chosen Advanced in previous years, choose this one

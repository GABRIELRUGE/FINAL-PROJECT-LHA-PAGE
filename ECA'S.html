EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Our students can participate in many extracurriculars

Students can also be chosen as yearbook editors, our Student Council

They can even volunteer at old folks' homes, Moisson Montreal (food bank)

Our administration, teachers and support staff (CCWs, Resource etc) are committed to helping all students achieve their goals

All of our teachers offer remediation during lunch hour, usually the second half, and after school. Students can also attend one of two homework programs, Step Up and Homework Program. A student must sign up for Step Up, but they can come to Homework Program Monday through Thursday without signing up. In both programs, teachers, CCWs and sometimes other students (volunteering) help them

MUSIC PROGRAM

CLASSES

At LaurenHill Academy, students can take music as a course from Secondary 1 to Secondary 5.

Each student has a chance to try different instruments, eventually choosing an instrument to play in the class.

BAND

Students may choose to join an Award-winning Jazz Band.

They will have the opportunity to compete with other schools from across Quebec and play in music performances.

TEACHERS
Mr. D. Ouellette Teaches at Junior and Senior Campuses

STREAMS
Intro To Music Music: Advanced
If your child has some experience playing an instrument (e.g. saxophone, piano, guitar, trombone) this is the one you should pick If your child has a lot of experience playing or is in Secondary 2+ and played in Advanced in previous years, choose this one
